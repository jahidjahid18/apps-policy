EasySync Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

EasySync ("we", "our", "the app") helps users sync local files and folders with Google Drive.

Information We Process

  • Google account identity (email, display name) for sign-in and account-scoped sync.
  • User-selected local folder paths and sync settings (stored on device).
  • Sync metadata (for example logs and state needed to run sync correctly).
  • Optional support/contact messages sent by users.

Google Drive Access

EasySync uses Google Sign-In and Google Drive API permissions to read, upload, update, and delete files according to user-selected sync mode and settings.

Local Storage

EasySync stores settings and sync history on your device to provide app functionality.

Purchases

One-time upgrade purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. EasySync does not process or store your card details.

Data Sharing

EasySync does not sell personal data. Data is shared only with providers required for functionality:

  • Google (authentication and Drive API)
  • Google Play Billing
  • Configured backend/support services for user-initiated requests

Security

Authentication is handled through Google Sign-In. EasySync does not collect or store your Google password.

Your Choices

  • You can sign out at any time.
  • You can remove connected Drive account and sync pairs from app settings.
  • You can disable background sync at any time.
