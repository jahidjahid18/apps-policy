Kids Monitor Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Kids Monitor ("we", "our", "the app") is a family safety application with parent and child companion apps.

Who Can Use the App

  • Parent or legal guardian accounts are responsible for child device setup.
  • Child device monitoring must only be used with required legal consent under local laws.

Information We Process

  • Account information (such as email and profile details) for sign-in and account management.
  • Device and pairing data needed to securely link parent and child devices.
  • Location data (live location, last location, movement history, safe area status).
  • Safety alerts and events (SOS alerts, geofence alerts, permission health, app-block events).
  • Optional profile image selected by the user.
  • Technical logs needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service.

Permissions Used

Depending on feature use, the app may request:

  • Location permissions (foreground/background) for live tracking and safety alerts.
  • Notifications permission for alerts and status updates.
  • Accessibility and usage access permissions for app-block enforcement features (Android).
  • Microphone permission for in-app audio calling (if enabled).

How We Use Data

  • To provide parental safety features, including live map, SOS, and safe areas.
  • To deliver alerts when configured conditions are met.
  • To maintain subscription-based feature controls and update intervals.
  • To protect accounts, prevent misuse, and improve reliability.

Data Sharing

Kids Monitor does not sell personal data.

Data may be processed by service providers required for app operation, such as:

  • Firebase services (authentication, database, storage, messaging, crash reporting).
  • Mapping/location providers used by the app.
  • In-app calling provider (Agora) for audio call functionality.
  • Payment/subscription platforms where applicable.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained only as needed to provide the service, support users, and meet legal obligations.
  • Parents may request deletion of account-linked data through support contact below.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; users should secure their devices and accounts.

Children and Family Safety

  • Kids Monitor is designed for family safety use by parents/guardians.
  • Parents are responsible for lawful use and proper consent before monitoring a child device.

User Controls

  • Parents can manage linked child devices, safe areas, and alerts.
  • Users can revoke permissions from system settings.
  • Users may contact support for account and data requests.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version is published on this page with the updated date.
